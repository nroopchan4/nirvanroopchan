Tenor pan

The tenor pan is really a beautiful instrument that originated in the caribbean and consists of many categories such as High tenor, Low tenor and Double tenor. The pans shown below in the mini gallary are all tenor pans not only do they look amazing and has multiple types but they all produce beautiful sounds also and can be used for any event ranging from music production to parties to carnival events and the list goes on.

The 6 bass pan

These pans known as the 6 bass pans(aka low bass pans)have the lowest range of notes in the steelpan family and are the largest of all the instruments.The lowest note is usually A below the c note in the 5 bass.
